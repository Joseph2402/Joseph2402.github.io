Attrition Dashboard

Executive Summary

The Attrition Dashboard is designed to provide comprehensive insights into the factors contributing to attrition, helping HR professionals and management to understand trends and patterns. The project utilizes data sourced from Kaggle, which was cleaned and transformed using Excel, and visualized using Power BI.

Attrition Rate Metrics:

Displays overall attrition rates, segmented by department, job role, salary hike, years at company, education etc.

Key Drivers:

Highlights major reasons for employee departures, such as compensation, job satisfaction, or management issues.

Interactive Visualizations:

Includes charts, graphs, and heatmaps for a detailed and intuitive exploration of data.
